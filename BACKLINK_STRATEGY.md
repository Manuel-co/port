# Backlink Building Strategy for manuchim.site

## 🎯 What Are Backlinks?

Backlinks are links from other websites pointing to your site. They're one of the most important SEO ranking factors because they signal to Google that your content is valuable and trustworthy.

## ⚠️ Important Note

**I cannot create backlinks for you** - they must come from other websites. However, I can provide you with a comprehensive strategy to earn them naturally.
